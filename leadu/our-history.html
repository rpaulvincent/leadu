Our History

Sun Drug Store, part of the SUN Group, specializes in marketing and distribution of branded pharmaceuticals and medical products. We import, support, and market prescription drugs, branded OTC, surgical equipment, medical supplies and consumer healthcare products ensuring the benefit of healthcare products reach to all those deserving to enrich their lives.

Our vision is to be the preferred pharmaceutical and healthcare products distribution company in Doha-Qatar with commitment to enriching lives through quality healthcare products and services. We also envision to be a leading brand in the industry by the year 2022.

Our distribution channel is one of the best in class enabling us to promptly meet the demand of our customers.

We have deployed structures and plans in all our distribution processes in order to ensure that we remain abreast of all technological developments in the industry; this ensure that we are able to give our customers our very best when it comes to serving them.

We have committed, competent and professional employees who have the knowledge and expertise necessary to our company and the industry. We intend to ensure that our employees undergo training on regular basis that will make them become more productive with up-to-date skill set.
